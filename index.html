Markdown-Centric Design

VuePress capitalizes on the simplicity of Markdown for content creation, enabling writers and developers to focus on their words rather than formatting.

Vue-Powered Customization

At its core, VuePress is enhanced by Vue.js, allowing you to embed dynamic Vue components within your static Markdown pages.

Optimized for Performance

VuePress generates pre-rendered static HTML for each page, ensuring that your site loads quickly and is readily accessible to search engines.
